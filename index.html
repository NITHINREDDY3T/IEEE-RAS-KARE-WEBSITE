About KARE

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE) is a renowned institution dedicated to excellence in education and research. Established with a vision to nurture intellectual capital, KARE provides a conducive environment for students to explore their passions and contribute to society's progress.

About IEEE RAS

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) is a prominent global organization that promotes the theory and practice of robotics and automation technologies. RAS fosters collaboration, knowledge exchange, and professional development among researchers, educators, and practitioners in these rapidly evolving fields.

By forming the IEEE RAS Student Branch Chapter at KARE, we aim to leverage the society's vast resources, connect with a global network of experts, and actively participate in shaping the future of robotics and automation. This chapter will serve as a stepping stone for our students to gain invaluable exposure, enhance their skills, and contribute to cutting-edge innovations in these dynamic domains.
